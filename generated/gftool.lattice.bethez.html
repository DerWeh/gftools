gftool.lattice.bethez¶

Bethe lattice for general coordination number Z.

In the limit of infinite coordination number Z=∞, this becomes gftool.lattice.bethe, in the opposite limit of minimal coordination number Z=2`, this is gftool.lattice.onedim.
